> > o TAXES THOUGHT EXPERIMENT
> > 
> >  1) I generate $100 of productivity for my company
> >  2) Company is taxed %30, $70 left
> >  3) Company pay shareholders and costs, $30 is left

> >  5) I pay 40% in taxes, so $18 left
