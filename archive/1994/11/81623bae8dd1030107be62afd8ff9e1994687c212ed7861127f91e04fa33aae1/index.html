  Digitale Burgerbeweging Nederland
..sticking up for the interests of digital citizens...

On Saterday 15 October the dutch db.nl (digitale burgerbeweging nederland)
organization was founded in Amsterdam. Some of our items are:

 o digital democracy, citizens' rights such as freedom of speech, 
   protection of privacy and involvement in policy making.

 o socially valuable applications of information and communication technology

 o an accessible and user friendly digital public network

The organization was founded in reaction of government plans to ban 
cryptografy, and regulation wich would forbid public libaries to lend 
electronic information. For more information you can check out our web-pages.
