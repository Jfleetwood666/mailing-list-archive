Ken Landaiche wrote:

> So honor may after all have meaning in cyberspace, as some code of
> behavior that preserves one's "reputation".

What is important is a _persistent_ and _unforgeable_ identity, not a
physical indentity. Persistence is needed to attach a history to, and
an expectation of future behavior. Unforgeability for obvious reasons.
