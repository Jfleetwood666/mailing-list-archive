Disney is a pretty paranoid company. I was there about 13 years ago 
with some friends and a couple of the people I was with decided to take 
a 'joint' break in the woods. They looked around and, not seeing 
anyone, ducked into the bushes. Within about 30 seconds several 
security guards came running and escorted them out of the park. The 
guards were not worried about what they were smoking, just that they 
had gone off the designated path. I've always wondered how the new so fast.
