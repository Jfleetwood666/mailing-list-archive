> Today in Houston at the District 6 State Senatorial Convention of the
> Democratic Party of Texas, as an initial step in the formation of the
> platform of the Democratic Party, a resolution was passed taking a stand
> against the program of a thousand names - Clipper Chip/Skipjack/Digital
> telephony bill.
> The journey of a thousand miles begins under your feet.  If all
> Libertarians, democrats, republicans and independents who oppose the

> a snail mail.
