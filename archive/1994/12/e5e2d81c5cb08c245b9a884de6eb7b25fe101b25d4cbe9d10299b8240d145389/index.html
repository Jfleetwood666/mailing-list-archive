(unencrypted) with many hops planned; a sample is included below. It would be
nice if the parties responsible would remove my address (and, for example,

if this is supposed to produce results representative of the entire remailer 
bramble. In the immortal words of Kibo, "PLEASE UPDATE YOUR COSMIC PARAMETERS."
