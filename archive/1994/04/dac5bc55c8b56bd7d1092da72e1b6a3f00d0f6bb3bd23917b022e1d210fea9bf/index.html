C'punks,

On Tue, 12 Apr 1994, Adam Lake wrote:

> . . .
> 
> A professor of communications has brought up the fact that it is illegal 
> to use a pseudo in the white pages of the phone book.  Is this the case?
> Help!!!!

To the best of my knowledge, your professor is full of it.  For years, I 
was listed in the White Pages as "TANSTAFFL."  The phone company put up a 
fuss at first, but I bullied them into it.

In the same White Pages a gay bartender in San Francisco's Castro 
district used the name, Kate Forna, which in telephone order is: 

			Forna, Kate

Works for me.
