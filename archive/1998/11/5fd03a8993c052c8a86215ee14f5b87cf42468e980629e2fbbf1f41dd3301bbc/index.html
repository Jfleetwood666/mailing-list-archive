Larry Dowling, an Austin attorney participating in CJ's
legal defense, has engaged me to gather information
for CJ's defense. This message and any related
correspondence with me is protected as "attorney 
privileged information." 

Larry, who does not use E-mail, has asked me to
obtain the following information for forwarding to
him (Plaintext and ciphertext welcomed; PK below.
Your choice as to posting responses to cpunks.)

1. How many people who saw the CJ posting cited by 
the IRS as a death threat against federal officials took 
it as a serious threat? See the IRS complaint at:

   http://jya.com/cejfiles.htm

2. If you answer "yes" to the question 1, then in your
opinion are you of sound mind?

3. How many people are willing to help CJ's defense by
answering questions and testify concerning his postings?

4. How many cypherpunks (and others) are willing to 
attend a trial for CJ in Washington State if the government 
proceeds to trial?
