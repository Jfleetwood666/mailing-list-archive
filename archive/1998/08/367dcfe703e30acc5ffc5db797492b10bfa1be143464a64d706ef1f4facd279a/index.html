Get Support Online Articles by E-mail
-------------------------------------

You can also receive these articles (and others) in e-mail by sending a message to mshelp@microsoft.com. In the Subject line of your message
enter the Article-ID number (Qnnnnnn). For example to receive Q162721
your Subject line should resemble the following example:

Subject: Q162721

You can have multiple articles sent to you in e-mail by typing multiple Article-ID numbers separated by a comma. For example:

Subject: Q178049
Q174914
Q174062

To receive an index of articles
enter "Index" (without quotation marks) in the Subject line. For example:

Subject: Index

The MSHelp Index is updated monthly. For more information about MSHelp
see http://support.microsoft.com/support/kb/articles/q183/1/21.asp.
