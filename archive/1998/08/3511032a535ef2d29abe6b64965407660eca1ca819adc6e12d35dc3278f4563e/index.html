Not to bug you personnally, just a comment on the general state of affairs:

IMHO, there is no such thing as "legitimate needs of law enforcement" that
could justify such a limitation of the freedom of the individuals who had
done
nothing wrong.

"legitimate" in our present context means to "counter things that are
presently illegal", notwithstanding that an immense portion of the
population,
which in essence does not try to get a free ride from some sort of law, do
not
agree at all with the said law.

Somehow, I think that it is time to emphasize our taking of the moral high
ground.
