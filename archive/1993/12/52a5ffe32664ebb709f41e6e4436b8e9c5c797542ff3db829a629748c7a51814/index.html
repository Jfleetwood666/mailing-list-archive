It *says* from president@whitehose.gov.

to deliver it a couple of times before succeeding.

then it went thru uunet, to delphi.com and then to bix.com...
obviously bix gets it's mailfeed via delphi. (they provide commercial
ip and usenet/mail feeds)

It was faked basically. A big clue is when it has "Apparently-To:" in
the header...
